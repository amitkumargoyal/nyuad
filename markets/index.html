Markets

Interactive tools for the course
Tool 1
Supply & demand — curve shifts
Explore how changes in income, substitute prices, input costs, technology, and other factors shift demand and supply curves, and trace the effect on equilibrium price and quantity.
Tool 2
Competitive equilibrium & surplus
Set the parameters of linear inverse demand P = a − bQ and supply P = c + dQ. See the equilibrium, and the consumer and producer surplus triangles, update in real time.
Tool 3
Price controls
Impose a price ceiling or floor and set the control price with a slider. See whether it binds, how much excess demand or excess supply it creates, and how consumer surplus, producer surplus, and deadweight loss shift relative to the free-market equilibrium.
Tool 4
Tax incidence
Apply a per-unit tax and see why the statutory side doesn't matter: identical prices and quantity whichever curve shifts. Watch the consumer/producer burden split track the slopes, alongside revenue and deadweight loss.
